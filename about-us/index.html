OUR STORY

The Artbeat Entertainment company started in summer of 2021 in Delhi and Gaya, Bihar. Initially, company had a vision: to facilitate the best quality content and complete technical support with a rigid standard of ultimate quality, craftsmanship and creativity, to the young and new influencers, the upcoming talent and simultaneously sensed an urge to provide them digital support. We create our own quality content and basically provide it for short movies, documentaries, videos and promotions. We facilitate the small influencers as well as renown actors and existing famous crews with our technical direction, production and complete shooting set up with all the equipments required and studios for making it a worth project. We also provide complete digital support to the same, on every possible social media platform and also provide them the hashtags, short promo videos and posters.

ABOUT FOUNDER AND EXPERIENCES

The Artbeat Entertainment was founded by Mr. Rahul Rasimo, lead director. Rahul has done his graduation from Asian Academy of Film and Television (AAFT) and has been outshining in his field. He has worked with “Six Sigma Films” production house, also collaborated with youtubers who have millions of subscribers like Jatin Sharma, Realhit, Fuddu Kalakar, Sam Khan, also worked with “The Visual Cinema”, a film production company. He has directed web series like “Virgin@21”, “First Draft” on MX Player, which was also nominated for Dada Saheb Phalke Award, he was also associate director in “Pie Book” which was again nominated for nominated for Dada Saheb Phalke Award.

Also, Rahul’s script of a short film has been streamed on Amazon Prime. Company also has connections with platforms like Amazon Prime, Zee5 films TV and MX Player.
